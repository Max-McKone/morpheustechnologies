Overview

  Oke is responsible for Digital Futures IT infrastructure and application environment. He brings over 20+ years experience to Digital Futures with a proven track record in the design and implementation of technology strategies to support dynamic start-ups and companies experiencing periods of hyper-growth. Oke began his career in software development, working for a number of leading IT consultancies where he specialised in the design propriety applications to support the adoption of new management information and ERP solutions. He is a devoted father and committed member of his local community, who enjoys Spanish cuisine and hopes to see Tottenham lift some silverware at some point.
